Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a baked, flat bread crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese (usually mozzarella), and various ingredients like meats, vegetables, and herbs, creating a savory, customizable meal known for its comforting, cheesy, and often crispy textures. Described as a "slice of heaven" or "melty, cheesy goodness," it's enjoyed globally as a convenient, flavorful, and versatile food.

This no-knead dough is a beginner-friendly recipe for delicious, homemade pizza crust.

Reviewers praise the dough's fast rise and easy handling.

Allrecipes member cicada77 says, "This crust turned out great. Great flavor and texture."

Ingredients

  • 1(.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 cups bread flour, plus more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided

Steps

      Gather all ingredients.
      Stir yeast, sugar, and warm water together in a small bowl. Let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes.
      A bowl containing a mixture of yeast and liquid with a spoon
      Stir 2 cups of bread flour and salt together in the bowl of a stand mixer. Add yeast mixture and 2 tablespoons of olive oil; beat with dough hook on medium speed until dough is soft and slightly tacky, about 8 minutes, adding up to 3 tablespoons more flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing after each addition.
      Pizza dough being mixed in a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment
      Cover and let rise at room temperature until doubled in volume, at least 1 hour or up to 2 hours. (Alternatively, cover and let rise in the refrigerator overnight.)
      Bowl of risen pizza dough resting on a countertop
      Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C) with oven rack positioned in lower third. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface. Shape into a round and roll into a thin crust. Place on a large parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza pan.
      Hands spreading and shaping pizza dough on a wooden surface
      Top with desired pizza sauce and toppings. Brush edges with remaining 1 tablespoon oil.
      Pizza dough with tomato sauce and mozzarella slices on top
      Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden and cheese is melted bubbly, about 12 to 15 minutes.
      A cooked pizza on a baking sheet with a thin crust tomato sauce and melted cheese
      Serve and enjoy.
